Our story

The Kikacookies Bakery first opened its doors for business in West Ukraine’s comfortable town Lutsk in early 2010. Back then, healthy homemade cookies were virtually unheard of in Ukraine. Today the company has five branches across Ukraine. It has also recently launched its first international branch in Poland and is well on the way to opening up further branches across the Middle East.

Alicia Sweet, the company founder, developed the concept for Kikacookies Bakery in response to what she saw as a lack of any places in Ukraine to enjoy the taste of truly authentic Europe-style baking.
